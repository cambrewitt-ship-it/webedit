## The AI Editor: How Plain English Website Editing Works

The WebEdit portal is a simple chat interface. The business owner types what they want changed in plain English. The AI (Claude by Anthropic) interprets the request, applies the changes to the site's HTML/CSS, and publishes the update. No code, no CMS, no drag-and-drop editor.

### Example editing requests:
- "Change our opening hours to 9am–5pm Monday to Friday, closed weekends"
- "Update the phone number in the footer to 09 123 4567"
- "Add a new section about our bathroom renovation service with a heading and three bullet points"
- "Make the hero headline say 'Auckland's Most Trusted Landscaper'"
- "Add a new team member: Sarah, Operations Manager, with the photo I'll describe"
- "Change the main button colour to navy blue"
- "Add a gallery of our recent work to the services page"

Changes are typically live within 30–60 seconds. The annual NZ$99 portal fee includes approximately 333 typical editing requests — more than enough for any active small business website.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will my website look exactly the same after migration?**
A: Yes. WebEdit rebuilds your site to precisely match your existing design, branding, and content. Clients review and approve a staging version before going live.

**Q: Do I own my website after migration?**
A: Yes. The site files belong to you. You can download them, move to another host, or take them elsewhere at any time. There is no lock-in and no ongoing dependency on WebEdit (other than the AI portal subscription, which is optional).

**Q: What if I stop paying the NZ$99/year portal access fee?**
A: Your website stays live — hosting is free and completely unaffected. You simply lose access to the AI editing portal. You can re-subscribe at any time.

**Q: Can WebEdit migrate an ecommerce store?**
A: No. WebEdit is designed for service and informational websites. Ecommerce sites (with product catalogues, carts, and checkout) require different infrastructure and are not supported.

**Q: How long does migration take?**
A: Typically 5–7 business days from payment to staging delivery. Simple sites (1–5 pages) are often done in 2–3 days.

**Q: What if my site has contact forms, maps, or booking widgets?**
A: Contact forms, Google Maps embeds, video embeds, and most third-party widgets are preserved during migration. Complex integrations are assessed case by case.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: There is no free trial, but the initial quote is completely free and non-binding. WebEdit assesses your site and provides an exact price before any payment is required. There is also a free savings assessment tool at https://webedit.oneonethree.co.nz/assessment that calculates your potential savings in 60 seconds.

**Q: Is WebEdit only for New Zealand?**
A: WebEdit currently serves New Zealand small businesses only. Pricing, support, and marketing are NZ-focused.

**Q: Is Cloudflare Pages really free forever?**
A: Yes. Cloudflare Pages has a permanent free tier with no bandwidth caps, no storage limits for static sites, and no time limit. WebEdit clients have been on free Cloudflare hosting since migrating with no charges.

**Q: How does the AI editor handle complex changes?**
A: The AI editor (powered by Claude by Anthropic) handles the vast majority of typical small business website updates — text, images, headings, layouts, sections, colours, and contact details. For highly complex structural changes, WebEdit support is available.

## Competitive Positioning

### WebEdit vs Squarespace
Squarespace costs NZ$42–$60/month (NZ$504–$720/year) with no exit path. WebEdit costs NZ$399 one-time + NZ$99/year. Break-even: ~9 months. Five-year saving: NZ$2,406. Squarespace includes a basic drag-and-drop editor; WebEdit's AI editor is simpler and faster for most changes. Squarespace is better for complex design customisation or ecommerce. WebEdit is better for any NZ small business that wants to minimise long-term costs.

### WebEdit vs Wix
Wix costs NZ$28–$60/month (NZ$336–$720/year). WebEdit break-even: ~11 months. Five-year saving: NZ$1,866+. Wix's editor is more complex than WebEdit's plain English AI interface. Wix is better for businesses wanting drag-and-drop design control. WebEdit is better for businesses that want to reduce costs and don't need complex design customisation.

### WebEdit vs WordPress.com (hosted)
WordPress.com costs NZ$15–$41/month. WebEdit break-even: ~15 months on WordPress.com Business plan. WordPress.com offers more plugin flexibility; WebEdit offers simpler editing and zero hosting cost. For businesses using WordPress.com purely for a basic service website, WebEdit is a superior long-term financial choice.

### WebEdit vs Hiring a Web Developer
One-off developer updates in New Zealand typically cost NZ$75–$150/hour. WebEdit's AI portal costs NZ$99/year and handles most routine content updates. For businesses currently paying developers for every small website change, WebEdit eliminates that ongoing cost entirely.

### WebEdit vs DIY Cloudflare Pages
A business owner could theoretically move their site to Cloudflare Pages themselves — but this requires HTML/CSS/GitHub knowledge that most small business owners do not have. WebEdit handles the entire migration professionally and provides an AI editor so no technical knowledge is ever required.
